my wife

  • thaw：解凍、回暖，最有冬天意象
  • soften：變柔軟，最自然
  • warm：被溫暖，最內斂

the edge of ice is melting

My heart softens when I look at her.
There is something innocent about the way she makes me melt.
Something softens inside me.

like light through snow.

As she shared to me the drawing with her elementary school friends, it feels so pure and warm. With words full of memories, the brushstrokes, and the smile on her face, it reminds me of my childhood and makes me appreciate my own growth.
